üéØ How it works

Draw simple icons on the canvas to navigate between sections. The AI model will recognize your drawing and take you to the corresponding page.

üöÄ Technicalities

The underlying model is a simple CNN powered by TFJS. Not a GPT wrapper!

üìö Tutorial

Step 1: The Canvas

The drawing canvas is located on the right/bottom side of your screen. It's a 224x224 pixel area where you can draw simple icons using your mouse or finger.

Step 2: Drawing Recognition

The small AI model recognizes six different icons: home, envelope, person, suitcase, book, and X. Draw clearly and simply for best results.

Step 3: Navigation Magic

When the AI recognizes your drawing with over 90% confidence, it automatically navigates to the corresponding section.

Drawing Tips

The house has a big, triangular roof; while the suitcase is very rectangular, not a square!
